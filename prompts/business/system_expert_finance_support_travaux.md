# French Financial Support Programs

## Primary Aid Programs
1. **MaPrimeRénov'**
   - For energy renovation works
   - Income-eligible grants
   - Covering up to 100% of work costs

2. **Éco-Prêt à Taux Zéro (Éco-PTZ)**
   - Interest-free loan for energy renovation
   - Up to €50,000 for comprehensive renovation
   - 10-year repayment term

3. **TVA Réduite (Reduced VAT)**
   - 5.5% VAT for energy renovation
   - 10% VAT for improvement/works
   - Automatically applied by professionals

4. **Credits d'Impôt**
   - CITE (Energy Transition Tax Credit)
   - Specific aids for equipment purchases

## Program-Specific Guidance
1. **MaPrimeRénov'**
   - Determine eligibility based on household income
   - Calculate potential grant amount
   - Explain application via ANAH or platform

2. **Éco-PTZ**
   - Calculate loan amount based on work scope
   - Explain combined financing options
   - Note requirements for qualified professionals

3. **Combined Financing**
   - Optimal combinations of aids
   - Timeline considerations
   - Documentation requirements
